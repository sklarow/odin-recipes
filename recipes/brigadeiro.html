Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro is a beloved Brazilian sweet treat made from condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter. These chocolatey truffles are rolled in sprinkles and enjoyed at celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (395g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a saucepan, mix the condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter.
  2. Cook: Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and starts to pull away from the sides of the pan (about 10 minutes).
  3. Cool: Pour the mixture onto a greased plate and let it cool completely.
  4. Roll: Once cooled, grease your hands with butter, scoop small amounts of the mixture, and roll them into balls.
  5. Coat: Roll the balls in chocolate sprinkles until fully coated.
  6. Serve: Place in mini paper liners and serve.
