Feijoada

Description

Feijoada is a traditional Brazilian stew made with black beans and a variety of pork or beef products. It's a hearty, flavorful dish that's often enjoyed with rice and orange slices.

Ingredients

  • 500g black beans
  • 200g pork belly
  • 200g smoked sausage
  • 200g beef (chuck or brisket)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Soak the Beans: Soak the black beans in water overnight.
  2. Cook the Beans: In a large pot, add the soaked beans, bay leaves, and enough water to cover the beans. Bring to a boil, then simmer.
  3. Prepare the Meat: In a separate pan, heat olive oil and cook the pork belly, sausage, and beef until browned. Add the onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  4. Combine: Add the browned meat and aromatics to the pot with the beans. Simmer for 1-2 hours until the beans and meat are tender. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve: Serve hot with rice and orange slices on the side.
